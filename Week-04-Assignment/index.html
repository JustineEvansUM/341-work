Chocolate Beet Cake Recipe1

Source: Martha Stewert Living, November 2011

Getting Started:

"You won't taste the pureed beets, but they make this cake extra moist and play up its deep chocolate flavor."

  • Prep Time: 25 mins
  • Total Time: 3 hrs
  • Servings: 8

Additional Recipes:

There are two additional recipes needed to complete this cake. See the end of the ingredients below.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium beets, trimmed, peeled, and cut into 2-inch chunks
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened Dutch-process cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • Salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup safflower oil
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • Vegetable oil cooking spray
  • Additional Recipe: Chocolate Glaze for Cholocate Beet Cake
  • Additional Recipe: Candied Beet Chips for Chocolate Beet Cake, as garnish
